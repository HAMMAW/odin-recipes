Lemon Mint Juice

This is a simple recipe of lemon mint juice that can be made using day-to-day ingredients from your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Sugar
  • Mint

Steps:

  1. First, fill the glass with the water
  2. Then, add a tea spoon of sugar to the water and stir the glass
  3. Squeeze the lemons with a squeezer into the glass and stir it again
  4. Finally, add the mint into the glass

Ramen Noodles with Scallops

This is a simple recipe of ramen noodles that can be made using day-to-day ingredients from your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Onions
  • Scallops
  • Water

Steps:

  1. First you need to boil the water
  2. Then, put your noodles in the water
  3. While the noodles are being cooked, you can slice up the onion
  4. Lastly, put the scallops and the onions in
